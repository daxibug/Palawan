Adventure Destinations

San Vic View Point

San Vicente Viewpoint in Palawan offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, including lush greenery and beautiful beaches. This viewpoint is a popular spot for photographers and travelers looking to capture the natural beauty of the area. Visitors can enjoy a serene atmosphere while taking in the breathtaking vistas of the West Philippine Sea and nearby islands. The viewpoint serves as a fantastic location for sightseeing and appreciating the unique ecosystem of Palawan.

Activities to do

  • Panoramic Photography
  • Bato ni Noynoy & Bato ni Ningning
  • 100 Steps Viewpoint
  • Scenic Lookout
  • Pair with Tours

Bato ni Ningning

Bato ni Ningning is a popular spot just a short distance from San Vicente View Point. Despite offering similar views, it remains a favorite among tourists due to its stunning sunset ocean views and climbable rocky outposts that make for great photo opportunities. There are two different places to stop, each requiring an affordable entrance fee. The area features a mini store and cottages where visitors can relax and enjoy the scenery without any corkage fees.

Activities to do

  • Photography & Scenic Views
  • Sunset Watching
  • Adventure Riding
  • Hiking/Trekking
  • Picnic & Relax

Inandeng River

surrounded by lush mangrove forest. Not a lot of tourists know about this attraction so make sure to inquire about it from your host. Here you can embark on a guided river cruise, led by a local, to explore the mangrove-lined waterways. This tour provides an excellent opportunity to learn about the importance of mangroves in protecting marine life River

Activities to do

  • Kayaking
  • Mangrove River Cruise
  • Nature Sightseeing

Hundred Steps

100 Steps is a viewpoint located near the center of San Vicente, easily accessible within 8 minutes by motorbike from the village center. The entrance fee is 20 pesos per person and it's about a 0.8km hike to the top. Visitors are greeted with a breathtaking panoramic view of the surrounding islands and beaches, making it a must-visit for nature lovers and hikers.

Activities to do

  • Panoramic Photography
  • Sunset Watching
  • Hiking/Walking
