How to get to Palawan

Puerto Princesa City, the heart of Palawan, is a paradise of natural wonders and cultural heritage. Known as the “City in a Forest,” it boasts pristine beaches, lush jungles, and the world-renowned Puerto Princesa Underground River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Visitors can explore breathtaking limestone caves, vibrant marine ecosystems, and diverse wildlife, making the city a top eco-tourism destination.

Honda Bay Island Hopping

Honda Bay is a must-visit destination in Puerto Princesa, renowned for its pristine white sand beaches, breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant coral reefs, teeming with diverse marine life.This tropical paradise offers a plethora of picturesque islands, islets, and sandbars that can be explored on a day trip. With numerous diving and snorkeling sites, visitors can immerse themselves in the vibrant underwater ecosystem of Puerto Princesa

Activities to do

  • Snorkeling & Diving
  • Banana Boating
  • Jet-ski & Kayaking
  • Fish Feeding
  • Taking photos at picturesque spots

Starfish Island

Starfish Island is one of the islands in Honda Bay, Puerto Princesa, Palawan. The name of the island is attributed to the island's surplus of starfish. The shallow waters around this small island are strewn with starfishes. Some starfish seemed to like hanging out in the sea, while others seemed to like hanging out on the sand. Snorkelers will love this island as there are no jellyfish and the fish that are swimming around which makes the activity seem much more relaxed.

The island is often known as a stopover for lunch too. So, when you're diving and snorkeling at the beach to see the fishes, your guide will prepare your lunch.

Activities to do

  • Starfish Encounters
  • Snorkeling & Swimming
  • Island Hopping
  • Photography & Sightseeing
  • Relaxing & Sunbathing

Luli Island

Luli Island is one of the many islands on Honda Bay located on the eastern shore of Puerto Princesa, Palawan. It is one of the more popular islands in Honda Bay included in the island hopping tour packages. The island got its name from the vernacular words: 'lulubog' and 'lilitaw,’ which means to submerge and emerge, respectively. During high tide, the tiny island submerges, and during low tide, it appears. Luli Island has three beautiful sandbars perfect for strolling and for taking amazing photos. Its clear emerald water is great for swimming or snorkeling. The island’s cream-colored and powder-like sand is ideal for sunbathing or just simply lazing around. Tourists enjoy fish feeding just off the beach as schools of fish swarm around their feet.

Activities to do

  • Cliff Diving
  • Snorkeling & Swimming
  • Photo Opportunities
  • Relaxation & Sunbathing
  • Sandbar Strolling
  • Beach Dining

Cowrie Island

Cowrie Island, one of the stunning stops on a Honda Bay island hopping tour, is a tropical paradise known for its powdery white sand, clear blue waters, and laid-back atmosphere. Located just off the coast of Puerto Princesa City, this picturesque island is the perfect destination for beach lovers looking to relax, enjoy water activities, or savor fresh seafood. As one of the most popular top tourist spots in Puerto Princesa, Cowrie Island offers a balance of relaxation and adventure, making it a must-visit for anyone exploring Palawan Island.

Activities to do

  • water Sports
  • Snorkeling & Swimming
  • Kayaking
  • Relaxation & Massage

Nagtabon Beach

Nagtabon Beach offers an unparalleled experience with its pristine golden sands and azure waters, framed by lush greenery and towering coconut palms. Unlike more tourist-heavy destinations, Nagtabon Beach remains relatively undiscovered, providing a tranquil retreat where you can unwind away from the crowds. During the monsoon season, Nagtabon Beach is an excellent spot for surfing, adding a thrilling dimension to your visit.

Activities to do

  • Surfing & Board Sports
  • Swimming & Snorkeling
  • Relaxation & Sunbathing
  • Sunset Viewing
  • Nearby Nature Exploration
