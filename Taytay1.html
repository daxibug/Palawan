Adventure Destinations

Fuerza de Santa Isabel (Fort Santa Isabel)

The Fort de Santa Isabel has a quaint chapel that radiates solemnity and a small museum displaying artifacts that played important roles in Taytay’s history. Travel back in time with an educational tour through the walls of this historic landmark of Taytay and enjoy the scenic view from the fortress. As one of the palawan activities, visiting the fortress will give a glimpse of the rich history of the island.

Activities to do

  • Educational Tour
  • Take Photos of the Architecture

Lake Manguao

The lake is only a 10 minute drive from the center of town, visitors can take a tricycle, a motorbike or a car out to the lake. From the drop off point, it is only a 3 km walk to the water's edge.The best way to sightsee around the bay is by boat. Ask your accommodation establishment or the tourism office at Fuerza de Santa Isabel about renting a kayak, or being transported around the lake.

Activities to do

  • Kayaking & Boating
  • Birdwatching
  • Camping & Hiking
  • Fishing
  • Nature Photography
