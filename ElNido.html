How to get to Palawan

Explore El Nido town on Palawan Island, a paradise of stunning limestone cliffs, turquoise lagoons, and vibrant marine life. Discover the best times to visit, insider tips, top attractions, and the best things to do in El Nido town. This guide covers everything you need to plan an unforgettable holiday in one of the Philippines’ most breathtaking destinations.

El Nido town is home to stunning rock formations, hidden lagoons, and pristine beaches. From island-hopping to snorkeling, there’s no shortage of activities and tours in El Nido town for beach lovers and adventure seekers alike. Recognized as one of the world’s top island destinations, this town will captivate you with its dramatic limestone cliffs, white sand beaches, and turquoise waters.

Island Hopping Highlights (Bacuit Archipelago)

Big Lagoon (Tour A)

Famous for emerald waters surrounded by towering limestone cliffs, best explored by kayak.

Small Lagoon (Tour A/D)

A hidden, tranquil lagoon accessed through a small crevice in the rocks.

Secret Beach (Tour C)

A hidden beach tucked behind limestone walls, accessed by swimming through a narrow tunnel.

Shimizu Island (Tour A/C)

A popular spot for snorkeling, known for its vibrant coral reefs and marine life.

Snake Island (Tour B)

Features a unique, long S-shaped sandbar connecting the island to the mainland.

Helicopter Island (Tour C)

Named for its distinct shape, offering white sand beaches.

Cadlao Lagoon (Tour D)

A tranquil, often less crowded lagoon on Cadlao Island.

Best Way to Explore

  • Tour A: Best for lagoons and kayaking
  • Tour C: Best for snorkeling and scenic beaches
  • Tour B & D: Better for relaxing, caves, and avoiding the heaviest crowds.
